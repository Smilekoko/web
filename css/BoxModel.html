Padding -内容周围的区域，填充是透明的

Border -围绕Padding和Content的边框

Margin -边框外的边距，边距是透明的

例子

This text is the content of the box. We have added a 50px padding, 20px margin and a 15px green border.

元素总宽度=宽度+左Padding+右Padding+左Border+右Border+左Margin+右Margin

元素总高度=高度+顶部Padding+底部Padding+顶部Border+底部Border+顶部Margin+底部Margin
